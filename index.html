Malia Sweet's Bio

Malia Sweet was born April 24th, 2003 in New Braunfels Texas. She attended a private school up till 2nd grade before being pulled to homeschool due to ADHD, dylexica and encephalopathy. She always had an over-active imagination since she was a child, which stayed with her even after she went into highschool. She began writing May 31st of 2016 and writes an average of 2000 words a day. She has been writing fiction for close to 6 years and has slowly began to work on several YA book series' to someday publish.
