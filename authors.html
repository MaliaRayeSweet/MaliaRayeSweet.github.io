Co-Authors and Partners

Laine Mitchell

Laine (who's actual name is Mitchell Eddings) graduated highschool a year early and plans to begin attending OTC in the fall of 2022. He enjoys reading fictional and non-fictional works, as well as listening to educational podcasts and taking history courses online. He began tagged along in Villain Chronicles in 2021 for fun until he began to see the true potential in it. Present day, he has plans to help write and publish the series with Malia Sweet, The original creator of the story.

Rhoman Hyatt

Rhoman Hyatt is a talented musician, chief and writer who started writing Villain Chronicles with Laine and Malia in 2021. As the story took off, he and Malia began plotting out future works and creating the first draft chapters. In early 2022, Rhoman decided to focus on those other works (and some of his own) to allow Malia and Laine to work on Villain Chronicles. He is currently a student at OTC and is often found tinkering with a song on his laptop.
