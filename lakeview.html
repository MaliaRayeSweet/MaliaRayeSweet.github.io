The Cast of Lakeview

Summary

The City of Lakeview had been without a theatre for decades until Kaz Normoyle finally woke from a coma. Not only had 6 years of his life had been wasted away but he was soon after diagnosed with MS. When realizing that his time may be running out, he decided to risk it all and follow his dream of creating a theatre. With help from Quincy Adams and Stacy Lynn Groves, Kaz was able to create his dream theatre and gather a group of 8 crazy strangers to help him. With the threat of death hanging over Kaz's head and their stage ready, the team set out fulfill Kaz's dying wish before time runs out.

Characters

Kaz Normoyle

Kaz Normoyle is the original creator of The Lakeview Theatre. With help from his dear friend Quincy Adams, he managed to gather a group of amateur performers, writers and musicians to make his own dream a reality.

Quincy Adams

Quincy is a college drop-out with a dream of performing on stage for millions of people. Although he often had the lowest grades in school, his acting skills are certainly an A+. He enjoys helping with music, being a young musician big dreams. With his peppy personality and corny dad jokes, he's easily one of the most beloved of the cast.

Anisty Finnegan

Anisty works at a local coffee shop, living alone with her German Shepherd, Max and parakeet named Tori. She spends most of her time preforming on stage or helping behind the scenes to put on an amazing performance. She is the sister of Michael and is often seen helping him with his scripts.

Michael Finnegan

Michael cannot help but cry with joy at a happy ending, or get deeply, DEEPLY, invested in hallmark movies. Sure, his friends at the Theatre may give him a hard time but he knows they mean well. Michael is one of the senior members of Lakeview Community Theatre, acting as the group's leading writer.
