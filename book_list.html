Writing Projects

* Several of these stories are still currently in the process of being written and/or plotted

Villain Chronicles (Series)

Villain Chronicles is a 5 part series that follows the adventures of Adryan Thorne and his "team" of villains. Throughout the 5 books, the characters travel around their world of Thera to find 4 ancient relics to stop the divine forces that threaten their world. This series is both a comedy and a science-fiction work that ties in humor with the question of "Who are the real villains?"

The Cast of Lakeview (Mini Series)

Lakeview Theatre is brought to life by 10 cast members who set their sights to the stage. This slice of life features an array of characters from all walks of life, coming together to create unique plays for the citizens of Lakeview and beyond. A simple yet addictive series full of humor, warmth and plenty of dramatics.
